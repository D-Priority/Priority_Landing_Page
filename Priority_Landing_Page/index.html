What We'll Need From You to Get Started

To avoid delays, here's what you should have ready, don't worry, we'll guide you if you're missing any of these.

Brand Assets

Your business name, logo (or an idea of your brand colors), and any existing designs.

Products & Services

A list of your products or services with prices and images, or willingness for us to help style this.

Content & Copy

Business description, About Us info, contact details, and social media handles.

Domain Name

A domain name preference (e.g. yourbrand.com), we can help you buy this if you don't have one.

E-Commerce Details

Your product catalog, delivery/shipping terms, and preferred payment method (Paystack, Flutterwave, bank transfer).

Membership & Subscription

Your membership tiers, pricing, and what members get access to, for membership or subscription sites.

Reference Websites

Websites you like, even if not in your industry, helps us understand your taste and design direction.

No logo yet? No proper product photos? No wahala, we can guide you or connect you with people who can help before we start building.

/FAQ

Questions Nigerian Business Owners Always Ask

I already have an Instagram/WhatsApp Business page, why do I need a website too?

Social media pages can be restricted, hacked, or shut down anytime, and you don't fully control them. A website is 100% yours, builds more trust, and works even when social media is down or slow.

I don't have a logo or good product photos, can you still help me?

Yes. We can guide you to affordable designers/photographers, or work with what you have and improve it during the design stage.

How long does it take to build my website?

Landing pages: 3-5 working days. E-commerce and membership sites: 5-7 working days, depending on how quickly you provide content.

Do you only work with businesses in Lagos?

No. We work with businesses across Nigeria, Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan and beyond. Everything is handled remotely via WhatsApp, call, and email.

How do I know you won't just collect my money and disappear?

We work with a clear process, written proposal, and staged payments, you only pay the balance after you've seen and approved the work. You're in control at every stage.

How much will I pay in total, is there any hidden charge?

No hidden charges. The price we quote is what you pay, split into two simple payments. The only extra costs are your domain name (~₦15,000-₦20,000/year) and hosting, after a year which we'll help you set up transparently.

Can I make payment in installments?

Yes. Standard is 60% upfront to begin work, and 40% on completion, before the website goes live.

Will my website work well on mobile phone, since most of my customers use phone?

Yes. Every website we build is designed mobile-first, since most Nigerian customers browse on their phones.

Can customers pay directly on my website?

Yes, for E-commerce and Membership packages, we integrate Paystack and/or Flutterwave so customers can pay with card, transfer, or USSD.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

Each package includes free revision rounds. After that, we offer affordable maintenance plans or one-off edit pricing.
